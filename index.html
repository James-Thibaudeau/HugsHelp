Welcome to HugsHelp!

How do we help?

Volunteers go out to our events and hug away! The donors they sign-up, pledge pennies per hug and we count the hugs given to reach the final donation amount.

Why hugs?

A hug can indicate support, comfort, and consolation. Hugging has been proven to have health benefits. One study has shown that hugs increase levels of oxytocin and reduce blood pressure.

About Us

HugsHelp is a not-for-profit fundraising company that raises money for a variety of charitable organizations. Our goal is to help communities through hugs!
