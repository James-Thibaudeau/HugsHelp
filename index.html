Welcome to HugsHelp!

Helping communities through hugs!
How do we help?

Volunteers and the donors they get, pledge pennies per hug and we count the hugs given to reach the final donation.

Why hugs?

Hugging is good for you! Studies show that hugs increase oxytocin levels and reduce blood pressure.

Wikipedia - Hug
Who can hug?

Young or old, big or small, anyone can volunteer to hug!

About Us

HugsHelp is a not-for-profit fundraising company that raises money for a variety of charitable organizations. Our goal is to help communities through hugs!
